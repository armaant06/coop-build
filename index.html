Training · PPL ×6

Every working set 0–2 reps in reserve. Top of rep range on all sets → add weight next time. Rest 2–3 min compounds, 1.5–2 min isolations. Log your top set: weight × reps.

Diet · —

Gut rules — low-FODMAP, no lactose
  • No garlic or onion, ever. Use garlic-infused olive oil (FODMAPs aren't oil-soluble), scallion greens, ginger, smoked paprika, cumin, oregano, basil, soy sauce, maple syrup.
  • Marinara → plain passata + garlic-infused oil + basil + oregano.
  • Wheat pasta → rice, potatoes, rice noodles, GF pasta. Sourdough is the one allowed bread.
  • Dairy: whey isolate only. Lactose-free milk. Hard cheeses OK. Regular yogurt/cottage cheese are out.
  • Fruit: banana (just-ripe), blueberries, strawberries, kiwi, orange, grapes. Skip apples, mango, watermelon.
  • Veg: green beans, carrots, spinach, zucchini (small), cucumber, bok choy, potato. Skip cauliflower, mushrooms, asparagus.
  • Creatine: 5g daily; split 2.5g ×2 with food if it bloats you.
Sunday prep script — 75 min
  1. Oven 425°F. Two sheet pans of potatoes (2.5 lb, cubed, oil + paprika + salt) — 35 min.
  2. Brown 2.5 lb ground beef with the spice mix. Portion 5 × 170g into containers.
  3. Rice cooker: 3 cups dry jasmine. Portion over the beef (200g cut / 300g build).
  4. Bake 2 lb chicken thighs (salt, paprika, garlic-infused oil) — dinners Mon–Wed.
  5. Frozen veg stays frozen — microwave day-of.
  6. Hard-boil 4 eggs as emergency snacks.
Grocery list — ~$85/wk

One-time: garlic-infused olive oil (~$9), soy sauce, maple syrup, spices. Whey isolate ~$40/mo ≈ $10/wk. Tight week? Chicken thighs replace half the beef.

Eating out — safe orders
  • Chipotle: rice + double meat + cheese + lettuce. No beans, no salsa.
  • Grilled chicken or steak-tips plates, sushi, hibachi with rice.
  • One flexible meal per week is built into the math. Don't track it — just return to the template at the next meal.
